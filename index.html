Overview

Simplified Overwatch describes how to play each Overwatch character, in only a few simple words.

Note: This advice only works with ranks lower than Master. After that, more extensive guides are required for each character.

General Advice

  • When seeking out enemies, don't just look left and right; also look up and down.
  • When dying quickly and often, consider playing closer to your team.
  • The right time to use ult is usually if the other team ults, or if you're about to die, not if the fight is over.
  • Wait for your team before going into a fight. If you're the only one left, try to escape.
  • Be sure to aim for the head.

Character-Specific Advice

Keep the other support alive.

Take up space, and don't die.
